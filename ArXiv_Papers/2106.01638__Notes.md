
# On the reciprocal sum of lcm of k-tuples

## Authors

- Sungjin Kim

## Abstract

We prove that the reciprocal sum $S_k(x)$ of the least common multiple of $k\geq 2$ positive integers in $\mathbb{N}\cap [1,x]$ satisfies $$ S_k(x)=c_k \log^{2^k-1} x +O(\log^{2^k-2} x) $$ where $c_k$ is a positive constant.
